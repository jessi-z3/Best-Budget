## Best Budget Overview

Best Budget was my way of getting out of a spreadsheet. I spent a lot of time and love on the spreadsheet, but I wanted an easier way to update my bills and have them repeat at set intervals. I wanted to continue what I loved about my spreadsheet:

- There's an input for current bank balance.
- I can mark bills as paid.
- Pay date compares to bill dates to determine what's owed.
- It calculates the projected balance after bills are paid.

Now I can also do the following:

- Automatically calculate next pay or bill date.
- View bills this pay period at a glance.
- Allow different pay dates for different income streams.
- Easily add new incomes or bills and remove existing ones.

This app was built entirely using SwiftUI and CoreData + Foundation. I love making mobile apps, but I am especially fond of Apple's ecosystem, so I built this for the iPhone with VisionPro also in mind.
